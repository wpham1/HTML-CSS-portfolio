PROJECTS.

RIGHT TO RULE

Right To Rule is an online multiplayer tactical card game that has players defend their base from ruin. Utilise spell cards to strengthen your units and take over watch-towers to attack from above. Destroy the enemy base to win.

Roles: Art direction, 3D modelling, Animation, UI design, Sooo much Card Art.

History: This game was developed for my team's capstone project at QUT for our Games and Interactive Entertainment degree.

SACRIFICIAL WOOL

Sacrificial Wool is a short puzzle game where the player controls a sheep and leads it to the exit of the level. The player must use the various settings controls to manipulate the lighting, volume and graphical quality to let the sheep overcome obstacles.

Roles:Game Design, Art direction, 3D modelling, Animation, UI design, Concept art.

History: This game was developed for a game jam: Ludum Dare 43 in 72 hours, and the theme was sacrifices must be made. Our 3-man team came 9th place overall and 3rd place for innovation out of 2500 submissions. Our most successful game jam.

NEOPOLITAN PANIC

Neopolitan Panic is a fast-paced crazy-taxi-like arcade game where you deliver ice cream to penguin customers before the ice cream melts.

Roles: Game Design, Art direction, 3D modelling, Animation, Concept art.

History: This game was developed for Ludum Dare 39 in 72 hours, and the theme was running out of Power. This was our second ever game jam, and one that I'm proud of just for doing it. Our team came 66th place overall and 39th place for graphics out of 2349 submissions. I really enjoyed the aesthetics of this one.

DEATH'S GRASP

Death's Grasp is a short visual novel where the protagonist makes attempts to sway the personification of Death to keep his daughter alive. Slight horror vibes and grim aesthetics is the direction we wanted for this game jam.

Roles: Writing, Art direction, UI design, Character Design and art.

History: This game was developed for Ludum Dare 44 in 72 hours, and the theme was life is currency. Our team came 99th place overall and 25th place for mood out of 2538 submissions. A lot of people enjoyed this one, there's even videos of people playing.
